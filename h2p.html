Game instruction




Welcome to GuV!

           The purpose of the game is to guess which celebrity is in the immage. There will be several levels, from easy to hard. Each level will have 10 pictures, some of them cropped, other blurred. Under the pictures there will be eighter 4 possible options or a blank box where the player will have to fill the name of the celebrity. If he can't guess it, he will have the possibility to use a "skip-button" which will get him to the next picture.
